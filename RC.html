There are two options: using the online issuance service 'hire visa' or visiting the Mokdong Seoul Immigration Office.
"Hire visa" advantage: don't need to visit in person to Immigration Office.
Disadvantage: You need to pay a processing fee of 10,000 won. If the documents are not prepared accurately, the application will be rejected.
"Immigration Office" advantage: Swift issuance is possible, the suitability of documents can be promptly determined.
Disadvantage: Advance reservation for the visit is mandatory.

Photos edited with Photoshop or photos taken by a camera with editing functions are considered forged and cannot be used as ID photos for an RC.
Only original electronic files taken at photo studios are accepted.
✔ ID photo taken within the last 3 months
✔ 3.5cm * 4.5cm size (Passport photo specifications)
✔ Must be on a white background and ears must be seen
✘ same photo as the Passport
✘ selife taken by a cellphone
