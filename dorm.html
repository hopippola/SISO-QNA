Frequently Asked Questions

You need to make the payment in cash. Overseas transfer is not possible.

The official check-in date is 24th-25th Feb at 10:00~17:30pm.
Although you can check-in after 25th, you will not be able to get a refund for the late arrival period.
You can also check-in until 21:00pm if you contact us previously.

There is a curfew from 1 am to 5 am.
There isn't shared kitchen in Residence hall, but there is one microwave on the first floor.
You can eat at the cafeteria for 5000 won.

It is possible, there is facility to keep packages you ordered.
